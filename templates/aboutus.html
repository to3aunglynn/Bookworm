  • Meet the Founder

    Mr. Ernest Hemingway

    Ernest Hemingway was a well-known American writer who lived in the 20th century. He wrote many famous books like "The Old Man and the Sea" and "For Whom the Bell Tolls." He lived an adventurous life, traveling to different places and even working as a war correspondent during World War I and the Spanish Civil War. Despite his success, he also faced challenges and struggles in his personal life.
